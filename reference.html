Origin

Why This
Exists

ORIGIN
A curiosity about how systems break
Not the vulnerabilities in code — but the vulnerabilities in trust. The gap between what a system is supposed to do and what it actually does.
THEN

Intelligent systems don't just execute logic. They reason. They infer. And they can be deceived — at the reasoning layer, not the network layer.

Protecting an AI system is not about firewalls. It requires trust propagation across every layer — from input validation to output reasoning.
